Fact sheet

Anchored is a doomscrolling intervention app for iOS and Android, designed to help people reclaim time from social media without resorting to shame, gamification, or aggressive blocking. It is built independently, takes no advertising, and stores no user data on its servers by default.

  • Founded: 2026
  • Headquarters: Independent, distributed
  • Team: Small, intentionally
  • Investors: None
  • Pricing: To be announced
  • Platforms: iOS (launching first), Android (to follow)

Typography

Fraunces is used for display text, hero copy, and statistics. Designed by Phaedra Charles and Undercase Type, available free from Google Fonts.

Inter is used for body text, buttons, and all interface labels. Designed by Rasmus Andersson, available free from Google Fonts.
