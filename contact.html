What we won’t do

We don’t have a phone number. We don’t have a live chat widget. We are not on social media in any active capacity. If a message arrives that wasn’t sent by you to one of these addresses, it didn’t reach us.

If you submit a privacy or data request, please send it from the email address associated with your Anchored account, if you have one. Otherwise, include enough detail that we can verify the request is yours.
