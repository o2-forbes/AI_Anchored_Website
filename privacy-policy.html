Privacy policy

Plain English. No tricks.

This document describes how Anchored handles personal information. It is written to be readable, not to satisfy a checkbox.

Last updated: 1 April 2026

Summary

Anchored is designed to need almost nothing about you. By default, your screen-time data, schedules, and reflections never leave your device. We do not run analytics, do not use third-party trackers, and do not sell or share data with advertisers.

If you choose to create an optional account, we store the minimum needed to let you sync schedules across your own devices, encrypted in transit and at rest.

What we collect, and why

If you only use the app

If you use Anchored without creating an account, we do not collect anything from you. The app reads your device’s screen-time data through the operating system’s public API, processes it on your phone, and stores all results locally.

If you create an account

You may optionally create an account using an email address or a sign-in provider (Google or Apple). When you do, we store:

  • Your email address. Used for sign-in and to recover your account.
  • Your schedules and goal. Encrypted before leaving your device, decrypted only on your other devices when you sign in.
  • Your sign-in timestamps. For account security only. Not used for analytics.

We do not store your screen-time numbers, your reflections, or any usage data, even with an account.

If you join the waitlist

If you submit your email on our website, we store it in a separate system used only to send you one email when the app launches, and one optional follow-up if you ask to be notified about Android. After that, your email is deleted unless you have created an account.

What we do not collect

The full list is on the What we don’t collect page. In short: no scrolling content, no reflections, no analytics, no contacts, no calendar, no location, no messages.

Who we share data with

We do not share personal data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. The only third parties involved in the operation of Anchored are:

  • Our hosting provider, who processes encrypted account data on our behalf.
  • The sign-in provider you choose (Google or Apple), if you sign in via one of them.

Both are bound by data-processing agreements. Neither receives any of your usage data or reflections.

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your data at any time. To do any of these, email privacy@anchored.app from the address linked to your account, or use the in-app data controls in Settings → Privacy.

If you live in the EU, UK, or California, you have additional specific rights under GDPR or CCPA, including the right to object to processing and the right to lodge a complaint with your data protection authority. We will honour all of them.

Children

Anchored is intended for users 16 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16. If we become aware that we have, we will delete it.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will publish the updated version here and email anyone with an account at least 30 days before the changes take effect. The "Last updated" date above will reflect the change.

Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, email privacy@anchored.app.
